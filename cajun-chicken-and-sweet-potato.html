Cajun Chicken and Sweet Potato

A fast and easy lunch favourite.


Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 2 garlic cloves (peeled)
  • cajun seasoning
  • salt and pepper

  1. Cover both chicken breasts in cajun seasoning.

  2. Place a garlic cloved on top of each breast, then airfry for 20 minutes at 180c.

  3. Peel and cut the sweet potato into chunks, before covering dousing with some oil, salt and pepper.

  4. Airfry the sweet potato for 15 minutes at 205c.

  5. Cut the chicken into strips and serve atop the sweet potato.
