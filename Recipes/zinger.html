Fakeaway Zinger Burger

A low calorie alternative to a beloved KFC favourite! Simple and quick to make in an airfryer, who wouldn't love a Zinger at home.


Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts (flattened)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 30g Chilli Heatwave Doritos
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garlic granules
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp onion granules
  • low calorie cooking spray

  1. Pre-heat airfryer to 180c and spray a baking tray with low calorie cooking spray. Place the Doritos and spices into a food bag and smash until they are nearly all in a fine crumb, but some larger pieces remain

  2. Ensure the chicken breasts are dry. Dust lightly with the cornflour. Break the egg into a dish and dip each chicken breast into it

  3. Put the chicken breasts onto the baking tray and sprinkle over the spice and Doritos mix, ensuring both sides of the chicken is coated thoroughly. Spray liberally with low calorie cooking spray and bake in the airfryer for 25 mins

  4. After 15 minutes, turn the chicken and spray with low calorie cooking spray again. Bake for a further 10 minutes until the chicken is completely cooked through and golden

  5. Serve with your choice of garnish!
